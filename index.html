Welcome to SKKU Interactive Data Computing Laboratory!

We aim to bridge the gap between humans and data. The ever-increasing size and complexity of data now challenge our perceptual and cognitive abilities, making modern datasets and AI models difficult to interpret without computational and visual support. Our research addresses this challenge by studying interactive visualization systems, drawing from the fields of Information Visualization and Human–Computer Interaction. We aim to help users explore, understand, and reason about complex data and models by tightly integrating computational methods with human-centered visual interfaces.

Check out our open-source projects and research prototypes at https://github.com/skku-idclab!
